# TypeMoor Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-17_

TypeMoor does not collect, transmit, or store any user data.

The app runs entirely on your device. It does not:

- create accounts or require login;
- make any network requests;
- access contacts, location, photos, microphone, or any other system permission;
- include third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising.

There is nothing to share, sell, or breach.
